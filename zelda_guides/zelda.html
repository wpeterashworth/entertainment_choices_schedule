Legend of Zelda Guides

All Projects Home Page Twilight Princess Ocarina of Time

Here you will find guides to two games: Legend of Zelda Twilight Princess, and Legend of Zelda Ocarina of Time. More games may or may not be added in the future. You can expect to find tips on where to find the pieces of heart, where to find empty bottles, how to progress in the story, and so on. Twilight Princess includes where to find bugs, and Ocarina of Time will include where to find the skulltulas.
